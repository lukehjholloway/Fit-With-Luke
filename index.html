My Story

From San Diego to the World

My name is Luke Holloway and I am a strength and performance coach. I played professional football and am also deeply involved in the ministry of the Gospel. I grew up right here in San Diego, CA — playing soccer, baseball, basketball, track, and football.

Football was my identity and the love of my life. My senior year, Idaho State University offered me a full-ride scholarship. I played as an ISU Bengal from 2015–2019, battled through knee surgeries, and came out the other side earning All-League Honors my senior year.

I went on to sign with the Canadian Football League (2022), got drafted to the XFL (UFL) 2023, and was honored to play overseas in Germany (2024). Along the way I've had Hall of Fame coaches, traveled the world, and fostered communities through ministry.

Today, my dream has expanded beyond the NFL. Now I want to serve you and help you pursue yours.
